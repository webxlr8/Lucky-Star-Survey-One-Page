Services

Services

Land survey

A land survey is the process of determining the precise boundaries of land.

Total Station Survey

Total station survey is a surveying method that uses electronic distance measurement instruments and a theodolite to collect angle and distance measurements.

DGPS survey

A differential GPS survey is a surveying technique that uses the principles of satellite triangulation to determine the precise location of points on the Earth's surface.

Digital Survey

A digital survey is a land survey that uses digital tools and technologies to collect data. This type of survey can be used to collect data about the land itself, or about objects on the land.

Contour Survey

Contour surveys are used to map the three-dimensional shape of the land surface. This type of survey is often used in conjunction with other types of surveys, such as property boundary surveys, to create a more complete picture of the land.

Setout Work

A setout is a plan or drawing that shows the precise location of something, such as a building, a piece of equipment, or a pipe.

Road survey

A road survey is an inspection of a road to assess its condition and identify any potential problems. It is a vital part of maintaining a road network, and can help to prevent accidents and damage to vehicles.

Building survey & documentation

Building survey and documentation is the process of assessing a building's condition and documenting its features. This can be done for a variety of reasons, such as to create a record of the building for future generations, to assess its suitability for renovation or demolition, or to understand its history.

Topography survey

A topography survey is a type of survey that is used to collect data about the physical features of a piece of land. This data can be used to create a map of the area.
